Help & Features

This page provides a quick guide to using the Bible App effectively.

Getting Started

  • Use the Home page to access daily verses and navigation links.
  • Choose your preferred translation (KJV or WEB) from the menu or links.
  • Explore highlighted daily verses for inspiration.

Searching

  • Go to the Search page to find verses by keyword.
  • Use the Search by Book page to browse verses from a specific book.
  • Each search earns you credits, which unlock extra features.

Credits System

  • Credits are earned by performing searches.
  • They are tracked by your email if you provide one.
  • Credits are virtual points — not real money — and cannot be withdrawn.

Offline Mode

  • If you lose internet connection, the app shows an Offline page.
  • You can still read cached verses and pages offline.

Support

If you need further assistance, please visit our FAQ page or Contact page.
