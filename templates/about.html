About the Bible App

The Bible App is designed to make scripture accessible, easy to explore, and available even when you’re offline.

Features

  • Read daily verses highlighted for inspiration.
  • Browse complete chapters and books in multiple translations (KJV, WEB).
  • Search by keyword or book name to quickly find passages.
  • Earn credits for searches, unlocking additional features.
  • Offline support — cached verses remain available without internet.

Our Mission

We aim to provide a simple, user-friendly way to engage with the Bible, whether for personal study, devotion, or sharing with others.
