Frequently Asked Questions

How do I earn credits?

You earn credits each time you perform a search. Credits are tracked by your email if you provide one.

What can credits be used for?

Credits unlock additional features in the app, such as extended searches or personalized verse recommendations. They are virtual points and cannot be withdrawn or transferred.

Can I use the app offline?

Yes! The app caches verses and pages so you can still read them when offline. However, new searches require an internet connection.

Which translations are available?

Currently, the app supports King James Version (KJV) and World English Bible (WEB). More translations may be added in the future.
